👥 Buyer Personas (4 Primary Segments)

1. Hotel Wellness Director

Pain Points: Staff turnover, guest experience gaps, revenue optimization. Priority: Consistent, reliable programming with zero operational burden. Budget: $2–$5K/month per property. Decision Criteria: Ease of scaling, guest satisfaction, revenue attribution.

2. Corporate HR Director

Pain Points: Employee retention, demonstrating wellness ROI (no longer a free pass in budget conversations), equity across demographics. Priority: Measurable outcomes + 91% of Gen Z say wellness is non-negotiable. Budget: ~$742/employee/year avg; structured programs deliver $1.50–$3 per $1 spent. Decision Criteria: Outcome data, utilization metrics, 70–88% engagement when well-segmented.

3. School District Wellness Coordinator

Pain Points: Student mental health crisis, compliance requirements, teacher burnout. Priority: Evidence-based programming — yoga/mindfulness proven to improve coping, attention, socio-emotional skills, and academic performance. Budget: $1–$3K/month (highly variable by district). Decision Criteria: Compliance fit, measurable student outcomes, teacher bandwidth preservation.

4. Healthcare/Clinic Administrator

Pain Points: Patient outcomes, practitioner coordination, insurance reimbursement. Priority: Integration with clinical workflows + measurable health metrics. Budget: Variable; tied to therapy services. Decision Criteria: Clinical outcomes, licensing compliance, patient satisfaction.
