Recommended Strategy: 3-Wave Approach

Wave 1: Expand Existing (Weeks 1-2)

Westin LB/Sac expansion, Dagny Boston case study, re-engage 14 "Contract Sent" leads, follow up 16 "Follow-Up Requested." These are warmest — highest close probability.

Wave 2: Local + Luxury Chain ABM (Weeks 3-6)

Fairmont Breakers pitch, Hyatt Regency LB pilot, Kimpton portfolio (50 emails ready), Four Seasons (22 emails). Target Spa Directors + Meeting Managers with personalized video outreach (Hedra).

Wave 3: Full Pipeline Re-Engagement (Weeks 7-12)

Enrich 339 missing emails via Clay/Apollo. Re-qualify 180 untouched leads + 313 "No Answer" leads with refreshed email sequences. Move schools to Kids GHL location.

Buyer Persona Targeting

Primary: Executive Meeting Manager / Catering Manager

Best entry point. Owns small groups, manages event space, evaluates vendors. Lowest barrier to a pilot. Message: "Zero-risk wellness activation for your dead space."

Secondary: GM / Hotel Manager

Overall guest experience owner. High leverage once pilot proves results. Message: "Measurable guest satisfaction lift without operational burden."

Tertiary: Sales Director

Owns occupancy gaps. Needs ROI stories and competitive differentiation. Message: "Hotels with wellness amenities generate 108% higher TRevPAR."

Market Segmentation Strategy

Convention Hotels (High Volume)

Room blocks in hundreds/thousands, F&B minimums focus, wellness = "gap-filler" between sessions. Entry: Wellness Gap-Fill Pilot ($1,500-$3,000). MYN handles instructors, insurance, QR check-in, reporting. Add-on: F&B voucher tracking.

Boutique/Lifestyle Hotels (High Margin)

Small groups 10-25, wellness as core experience, flexible space. Entry: Recurring programming partnerships. Boutiques are most creative in wellness — ideal for Yoga Bio Sync premium upsell and personalized protocols.
