Sales Angle — What Works

🎯 Key Insight from Barry + Jason (Hotel Operators)

1) Lead with ROI / revenue, not "wellness" as a vibe. Hotels get pitched constantly — "guest satisfaction" alone doesn't cut through. The winning angle is revenue generation.

2) Your real "in" is NOT the GM. GMs delete most cold emails. Start with the Director of Food & Beverage or Events/Programming leader — they're revenue-incentivized and can sell it upward.

3) Package yoga as an "activation + F&B" engine. The hotel sees it as a way to drive ancillary spend, not just a guest amenity.

Sprint Deliverables (Definition of Done)

1) Pitch Deck Rebuild

Page-by-page deck outline (4 pages) + one assumptions sheet (single source of truth). Numbers on page 1, pricing explicit, proof simple + believable.

2) Rate Benchmark + Unit Economics

Benchmark table by region/tier/modality. Unit economics template: instructor cost + travel + admin. Gross margin per class and per month.

3) ROI Model v1

ROI calculator with labeled assumptions, low/base/high ranges. Output: monthly incremental revenue range, break-even threshold, "what must be true" list.

4) Offer Packaging + Pricing Ladder

Package ladder: Pilot (2–4 weeks), Retainer (monthly), Add-ons (events, sponsorship, premium instructors).

5) Brand Audit + Luxury System

Website/brochure teardown. Luxury ruleset: typography, spacing/grid, image style. Shot list + locations + 1-day shoot plan.

6) Deck Production

Deck v1 complete with review checklist. Numbers on page 1, pricing explicit, execution looks low-friction for hotel.

7) Contractor SOP v0.1

Onboarding checklist, class standards, cancellation/sub policy, payments, incident handling, COI/waiver basics.

8) "Pilot in a Box" + Reporting

Week-by-week plan, roles, guest comms, measurement framework. Sprint 2 backlog ready.

Hotel Scoring Framework DIKSHA

Repeatable, explainable way to prioritize which hotels MYN targets

Scoring Dimensions

🏋️ Wellness Fit

Does the hotel already have wellness programming? Spa? Fitness center? Guest profile alignment with yoga/wellness.

🍹 F&B Activation Potential

Post-class upsell opportunity. Poolside bar, juice bar, brunch programming. Low-traffic hour activation.

📸 Social / Content Leverage

Instagram-worthy spaces, influencer potential, review volume. Brand amplification opportunity.

📋 Procurement Readiness

Vendor onboarding ease, contract complexity, decision-maker accessibility. COI/insurance requirements.

💰 Revenue Potential

ADR, occupancy, resort/destination fee context, group sales volume. RevPAR and TRevPAR indicators.

🎯 Strategic Value

Brand name recognition, portfolio effect (Westin → more Westins), geographic expansion, reference customer value.
