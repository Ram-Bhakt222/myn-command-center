👥 Buyer Personas (4 Primary Segments)

1. Hotel Wellness Director

Pain Points: 62% of hospitality employees report high work-related stress (burnout → turnover → guest-sat drop is the chain they feel). Staff turnover, guest experience gaps, revenue optimization. Priority: Consistent, reliable programming with zero operational burden; integrated wellness ecosystems. Budget: $2–$5K/month per property. Decision Criteria: Ease of scaling, guest NPS, RevPAR attribution, "Chief Wellness Officer" KPIs now appearing in org charts.

2. Corporate HR Director

Pain Points: Employee retention, demonstrating wellness ROI (no longer a free pass), equity across life stages (younger = mental/emotional, mid-career = caregiving, older = sustainable health). Priority: Hybrid-ready programs (on-site + remote), whole-person coverage, mental fitness (preventive) over crisis response. 91% Gen Z = non-negotiable; 95% workers view wellbeing domains as interconnected. Budget: ~$742/employee/year avg; structured programs deliver $1.50–$3 per $1. Decision Criteria: Outcome data, 70–88% engagement with segmentation, monthly impact reporting (58% of CEOs receiving monthly updates increased funding last year).

3. School District Wellness Coordinator

Pain Points: Student mental-health crisis, compliance requirements, teacher burnout; wellness programs are first-in-line for lean-budget cuts. Priority: Evidence-based programming; yoga/mindfulness proven for coping, attention, SEL, and academics. Budget Unlock (2026): CYBHI Multi-Payer Fee Schedule — all LEAs now eligible to bill Medi-Cal + commercial plans via third-party admin for school-based behavioral health services (meaningful for CA districts). Wellness days/leave approved for 2025–26 in many districts. Decision Criteria: Compliance fit, measurable student outcomes, teacher bandwidth preservation, framing as cost-saving (absenteeism ↓, turnover ↓).

4. Healthcare/Clinic Administrator

Pain Points: Patient outcomes, practitioner coordination, insurance reimbursement. Priority: Integration with clinical workflows + measurable health metrics. Budget: Variable; tied to therapy services. Decision Criteria: Clinical outcomes, licensing compliance, patient satisfaction.
